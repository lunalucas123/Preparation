Alaskan Klee Kai

Luna

Luna is an Alaskan Klee Kai is a spitz-type breed of dog, developed in the late 20th century
as a companion-sized dog sembling the larger Alaskan Husky. It is an energetic, intelligent dog
with an appearance that reflects its northern heritage. Bred in three sizes, its weight range is
from 5 to 45 pounds as an adult.
